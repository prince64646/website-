YAM GAUTAM'S SCHOOL

Location: Patandhoka, Lalitpur
Affiliation: National Examination Board (NEB)
Programs Offered: Management, Humanities, and Education
History: Established in 1981 B.S., it is one of the oldest and reputed educational institutions in Nepal.
Facilities: The school has been rebuilt with assistance from the Japanese government after the 2072 B.S. earthquake. It now features three blocks, including a retrofitted Rana-era conference hall and two newly constructed buildings, totaling 27 rooms. Facilities include laboratories, a library, teachers' rooms, administrative offices, and adequate water supply in bathrooms and hand-washing stations.
